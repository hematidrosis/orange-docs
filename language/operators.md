# Operators

The following are the operators in Orange: `+`, `-`, `*`, `/`, `++`, `--`, `+=`, `-=`, `*=`, `/=`, `%`, `&&`, `||`, `and`, `or`, `xor`, `^`, `&`, `|`, `<`, `>`, `-`, `!`, `>=`, `<=`, `==`, `!=`

Note that `&` and `|` are bitwise operators, and `&&` and `||` are the boolean ones. `and` and `or` are aliases to `&&` and `||` respectively.
